## Google Ads Setup

### Requirements for Automotive Advertising:
1. **Google Merchant Center** (for Shopping ads)
   - Not applicable - we don't sell products directly
   
2. **Google Ads Account**
   - Create at https://ads.google.com
   - Set up billing with business credit card
   - Create Search campaigns for keywords like:
     - "auto parts comparison"
     - "where to buy [part name]"
     - "cheapest [car part]"
     - "[car model] parts"

3. **Conversion Tracking**
   - Track affiliate link clicks as conversions
   - Track Pro subscription signups
   - Track email signups

### Recommended Campaign Types:
- **Search Campaigns**: Target people searching for specific parts
- **Performance Max**: Automated across all Google properties
- **Display Remarketing**: Bring back visitors who didn't convert

### Budget Recommendation:
- Start with $10-20/day
- Focus on high-intent keywords
- Monitor cost-per-click and conversion rate
- Scale up what works

### LSA (Local Services Ads):
- **Not applicable** for GarageBot as we're not a local service provider
- LSA is for plumbers, electricians, mechanics - businesses that dispatch to locations
- Standard Google Ads is the right choice for us
