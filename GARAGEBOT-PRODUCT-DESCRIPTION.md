# GarageBot — Right Part. First Time. Every Engine.

## Platform Overview

GarageBot is a full-stack AI-powered parts aggregator and vehicle management platform built by DarkWave Studios LLC. It unifies inventory from 97 retailers into a single searchable interface, serving casual DIYers, professional mechanics, fleet managers, and hobby enthusiasts across ALL motorized vehicle categories.

## By the Numbers

- **111,000+** lines of code
- **50+** pages / routes
- **436+** API endpoints
- **134** database tables
- **94** integrated retailers across 20 categories
- **54** active affiliate partnerships across 9 networks
- **58** React components
- **Full PWA** with offline support

## Core Platform Features

### Search & Discovery
- Universal parts search across 97 retailers (AutoZone, O'Reilly, RockAuto, Amazon, NAPA, Advance Auto Parts, and more)
- 20 vehicle/equipment categories: Cars, Trucks, Motorcycles, ATVs/UTVs, Boats/Marine, RVs, Powersports, Heavy Equipment, Agricultural, Aviation, Electric Bikes & Scooters, Go-Karts, Snowmobiles, Jet Skis, Lawn & Garden, Power & Energy, Interior & Accessories, Tools & Garage, Performance & Racing, OEM Parts
- Vehicle-aware search with Year/Make/Model/Engine filtering
- OEM genuine parts directory with 12+ manufacturer brands
- Price comparison and local pickup availability

### AI-Powered Intelligence (Buddy AI)
- Conversational AI assistant powered by OpenAI GPT-4o-mini
- AI Symptom Diagnosis Engine — describe problems in plain English, get ranked diagnoses with parts lists, DIY difficulty ratings, urgency levels, and estimated savings vs. shop repair
- AI-generated DIY repair guides with step-by-step instructions and video links
- Intelligent part recommendations and compatibility suggestions
- Mechanic cost estimates and proactive vehicle alerts
- AI part definition lookup

### Vehicle Fleet Management
- Full garage with unlimited vehicles
- VIN decoding for automatic vehicle identification
- Service history tracking and maintenance scheduling
- Mileage tracking
- Vehicle sharing between users
- NHTSA recall checker integration

### E-Commerce & Payments
- Stripe-integrated checkout with Stripe Elements
- Shopping cart with multi-vendor support
- Peer-to-peer Parts Marketplace (buy/sell used parts with marketplace fees: 10% free users, 6% Pro users)
- Pro subscription ($4.99/month) and Ad-Free tier ($5/month)

### TORQUE — Shop Management OS
- Standalone PWA for professional auto repair shops
- 5-step onboarding flow (Shop Info → Team → Services → Integrations → Verify)
- Digital inspections, repair orders, estimates, and appointments
- Technician time tracking and shop inventory management
- Customer management and review system
- Trust Layer blockchain-verified shop identities
- Marketing Hub with Meta/Facebook auto-posting and ad campaign management
- Business integrations hub: QuickBooks, ADP, Gusto, Google Calendar, PartsTech, and more
- Partner API system with API Key + Secret authentication and granular scopes

### Community & Social
- Signal Chat — real-time community messaging with WebSocket support and Buddy AI bot
- Shade Tree Mechanics — DIY community hub with categorized guides and savings estimates
- Break Room Hub — motorsports news, tools (receipt scanner, mileage tracker, maintenance scheduler), fuel price finder, speed trap alerts, CDL schools directory
